# 📦 Supply Chain Data Analytics & Optimization  

## 🚀 Project Overview  
This project focuses on analyzing supply chain operations to uncover inefficiencies, reduce costs, and improve decision-making using data.  

The analysis covers key areas such as **inventory management, warehouse performance, supplier efficiency, and profitability**, making it a real-world business-oriented analytics project.  

---

## 🎯 Objectives  
- Identify warehouses with high stockout risk  
- Analyze delivery delays and operational inefficiencies  
- Evaluate cost vs revenue performance  
- Understand demand and inventory gaps  
- Measure supplier performance  
- Optimize overall supply chain operations  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies  
- **Excel** – Data Cleaning & Preparation  
- **Python** – Data Transformation  
- **SQL** – Advanced Data Analysis  
- **Power BI** – Dashboard & Visualization  

---

## ⚙️ Methodology  

### 🔹 Data Preparation  
- Cleaned and standardized raw supply chain dataset  
- Handled missing values and inconsistencies  

### 🔹 Data Transformation  
- Structured dataset for analysis using Python  

### 🔹 Data Analysis (SQL)  
Performed advanced queries to identify:  
- High stockout risk warehouses  
- Delivery delays across warehouses  
- Cost vs sales imbalance  
- Warehouse capacity utilization  
- Demand vs inventory mismatch  
- Profitability across products and warehouses  
- Supplier performance metrics  

### 🔹 Visualization (Power BI)  
- Created interactive dashboards  
- Visualized KPIs like delays, costs, profits, and utilization  

---

## 📊 Key Findings  

- 🚨 **Stockout Risk:** Several warehouses face high stockout probability with negative inventory levels  
- ⏳ **Delivery Delays:** Certain warehouses consistently perform worse than average  
- 💸 **Cost Inefficiency:** Some locations incur high logistics cost but generate low sales  
- 📉 **Profitability Issues:** Specific warehouse-product combinations are loss-making  
- ⚖️ **Demand vs Supply Gap:** Imbalance exists across product categories  
- 🚚 **Supplier Impact:** Supplier delays significantly affect operations  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations  

- Improve **inventory planning** to reduce stockouts  
- Optimize **warehouse operations** to reduce delays  
- Focus on **high-performing locations** for expansion  
- Re-evaluate **loss-making product categories**  
- Strengthen **supplier selection and monitoring**
